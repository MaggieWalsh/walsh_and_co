About

Who we are:
We are a family run interior design business, based in Donnybrook, Dublin. Daniel TS Walsh founded Walsh & Co. in 1989 after many years in the design industry. We are now a 20 - strong team of designers and craftsmen.
What we do:
We take great care in selecting the right design for everyone that we work with. We source beautiful bespoke furniture and lighting to ensure a bespoke finish. We even have our own range of paint to choose from.

At Walsh & Co. your safety comes first.
For this reason we are limiting our appointment system to only three customers in the showroom at any one time. For a dedicated one to one appointment with a member of our interior design team, please book here.
