About

Who we are:

We are a family run interior design business, based in Donnybrook, Dublin. Daniel TS Walsh founded Walsh & Co. in 1989 after many years in the design industry. We are now a 20 - strong team of designers and craftsmen. Run by Daniels daughter, Meaghan, pictured above.


What we do:

We take your project from conception to completion, and manage everything along the way. We take great care in selecting the right design for everyone that we work with. We source beautiful furniture and lighting to ensure a bespoke finish. We even have our own range of paint for you to choose from.

At Walsh & Co. your safety comes first.
For this reason we are limiting our appointment system to only three customers in the showroom at any one time.
